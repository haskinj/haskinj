### 🏛️ The Master Hub
I build open-source tools to help people navigate **cognitive friction**, **systems exhaustion**, and **human hardware management**. Most of my work revolves around the **DodecaGone Systems** framework—a multidisciplinary research project that treats the human body and mind as a single, highly integrated system.

If you are experiencing acute overwhelm, sensory friction, or burnout, it usually isn't a moral failure. It's a **physics problem**, and physics problems can be solved with the right mechanical protocols.
