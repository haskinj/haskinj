# Hi, I'm Jesse.

I build open-source tools to help people navigate cognitive friction, exhaustion, and systems management. 

Most of what I do revolves around **DodecaGone Systems**—a framework that treats the human body and mind as highly integrated hardware. If you're stressed, burned out, or feeling stuck, it usually isn't a moral failure. It's just a physics problem, and physics problems can be solved.

My goal is to provide robust, simple, and precise toolkits that anybody can use to stabilize their life and get back to a functional baseline. 

---

### 🧰 The Toolkits
All my work operates under the `CC BY-SA 4.0` license. Please grab what you need.

*   **[You Are Here](https://github.com/haskinj/you-are-here)** — A crisis stabilization toolkit. If you are experiencing acute overwhelm or panic, start here. It provides immediate grounding mechanics and a simple scale to measure where your head is at.
*   **[I Need Maintenance](https://github.com/haskinj/i-need-maintenance)** — The daily mechanics. A basic diagnostic and repair manual for human systems to keep you running smoothly after a crisis passes.
*   **[Forge Math](https://github.com/haskinj/forge-math)** — The underlying engine. A mathematical framework mapping lived human experience over the top of actual thermodynamic and physics structures.
*   **[CR-IMRaD](https://github.com/haskinj/cr-imrad)** — A highly structured writing standard designed to make dense technical research readable for everyone without sacrificing the science.
